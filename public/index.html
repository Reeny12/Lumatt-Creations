How to place an order

To place an order with Lumatt Creations, simply send us a message on WhatsApp including an image of the outfit you would like to order, along with details on how you would like it customized. Once we have reviewed your request and confirmed that we can fulfill your order, we will ask you to make a deposit to proceed. You will then have the option to choose between a virtual or in-person measurement session to ensure a perfect fit.
